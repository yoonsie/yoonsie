Reason

I'm 8 years old scared out of my mind, holding the corded telephone by my ear in the living room. The phone is ringing and I am waiting nervously, secretly hoping they won't pick up. My hands are sweaty. Knees weak, arms heavy. No mom's spaghetti. I have to order pizza for the family.

It may surprise some friends who met me in adulthood, but I was near-crippingly shy growing up.
