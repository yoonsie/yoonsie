How to make an analog zettelkasten for overthinkers

Reason

I made an analog zettelkasten, or Antinet (Analog, Numeric-alpha, Tree, Index), so that I can better retain content, organize concepts, and generate new ideas based on newfound connections. I followed the LEAN principle and used a shoebox. Consistency is hard for me, but I'm hoping in a year, this zettelkasten will greatly benefit me.
